GDPR Compliance Policy – TalentLix OÜ (Estonia)

TalentLix OÜ, established in Estonia, is committed to protecting your personal data and processing it in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – Regulation (EU) 2016/679). By registering and completing your profile, you acknowledge and consent to the following:

1. Purpose of Processing

Your data is collected and processed solely for the purpose of talent discovery, sports career opportunities, networking, and related services provided by TalentLix.

2. Data Categories

The personal data processed may include, but is not limited to:

  • Identification data (name, nationality, birth date)
  • Contact details (email, phone, residence)
  • Professional and sports information (sport, role, career history, achievements)

3. Data Sharing

Your data will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent, unless required by law or necessary to provide the services of the platform.

4. Storage & Retention

Data will be stored securely on servers located within the European Union. Your personal data will be retained for as long as your account remains active or as required by applicable law.

5. Rights of the Data Subject

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct or update it
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Restrict processing or request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

6. Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before consent was withdrawn, but may limit your ability to use certain features of the platform.

7. Contact

For any questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@talentlix.com.

By ticking the box below in the registration process, you confirm that you have read and understood this GDPR Compliance Policy and agree to the processing of your personal data by TalentLix OÜ in accordance with the terms above.
